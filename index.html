About Me

My name is Lilly Leiran. I'm currently a student in a Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp learning front and backend web development tools. We started with front-end web development and so far I've learned HTML, CSS, front end JavaScript, JQuery, Bootstrap & Bulma, Web APIs, Third-Party APIs, and Server-Side APIs. Now we are starting to learn backend web development. I will graduate in May 2022.

I decided to do this bootcamp because I am in a boring, dead-end, low ceiling job and I don't want to spend the rest of my working life there. I am a person who loves to learn, nothing makes me happier at work than being challeneged to learn new things, think in new ways, and provide creative solutions. I love to multi-task, I love working within the constraints of what others need from my solution to find something that works for everyone, and I love to dig into the fine details of a problem to find a creative, simple solution. And, from my experience with coding so far, coding ticks all of those boxes. I will always have more available to me to learn and new projects to challenge me.

Other than work and my coding bootcamp, I love listening to books and podcasts, writing, traveling, spending time with my boyfriend, my friends, and my family, watching movies and TV, cooking, crocheting, sewing, hiking, weight-lifting, yoga, boxing, and I love making my podcast Story Therapy Podcast. So far I’ve released 1 season of my podcast, 20 episodes between July and November 2021, I’ll be back for season 2 after my bootcamp is complete sometime this summer.

Below you'll find some of my work so far, I will keep updating this as I accumulate more. Thank you for visiting my portfolio and please reach out with any questions.
